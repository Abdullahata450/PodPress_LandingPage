Features

Automate your content curation pipeline

📡

Feed Monitoring

PodPress continuously monitors your connected RSS feeds around the clock, detecting new articles the moment they publish. Set polling intervals from real-time to daily and never miss a piece of content from your favorite sources.

🎯

Smart Curation

Our AI analyzes incoming feed items and scores them based on relevance, trending potential, and alignment with your newsletter's editorial focus. It surfaces the best content so you can build each edition in seconds instead of hours.

⏰

Auto-Scheduling

Define your newsletter cadence and let PodPress handle the rest. It assembles curated content into your template and queues it for delivery at the optimal send time based on your subscriber engagement patterns.

🔍

Content Filtering

Set up keyword rules, topic filters, and exclusion lists to control exactly what makes it into your newsletter. Fine-grained filters ensure only high-quality, on-brand content reaches your audience.

🔗

Multi-Source Aggregation

Connect hundreds of RSS feeds from blogs, news outlets, podcasts, and niche publications into a single unified stream. PodPress deduplicates overlapping stories and merges related items for a clean editorial view.

📬

Delivery Tracking

Monitor every send with real-time delivery reports, bounce tracking, and inbox placement data. Identify deliverability issues before they impact your subscriber base and maintain a healthy sender reputation.
