Podcast to Blog

Convert spoken conversations into SEO-optimized blog posts with one click

The Process

Three steps from audio to published post

🎙️
Step 1

Recording to Text

Upload your podcast episode in any audio format and our speech-to-text engine transcribes it with over 98% accuracy. Speaker diarization separates each voice, timestamps are preserved, and filler words are automatically stripped so you start with a clean, readable transcript ready for transformation.

📐
Step 2

Smart Formatting

The AI restructures your transcript into a proper blog post with an engaging introduction, logically ordered sections, and a compelling conclusion. It generates subheadings, inserts paragraph breaks at natural topic shifts, and formats quotes and callouts to create a post that reads like it was written from scratch.

🚀
Step 3

SEO Optimization

Before publishing, PodPress runs a full SEO analysis on your post. It generates meta titles, descriptions, and Open Graph tags, suggests internal linking opportunities, and scores your content for keyword density and readability. Your blog post is primed to rank from the moment it goes live.

Capabilities

Built for podcasters who want to grow organically

🤖

AI Transcription

State-of-the-art speech recognition handles accents, technical jargon, and cross-talk with ease. The engine learns from your corrections over time, delivering increasingly accurate results with every episode you process.

📑

Heading Generation

Automatically generates hierarchical H2 and H3 headings based on topic analysis of your conversation. Each heading is crafted to be both reader-friendly and keyword-rich for optimal search engine visibility.

🖼️

Image Suggestions

Receive contextually relevant image suggestions for each section of your blog post. PodPress recommends stock photos, generates placeholder graphics, and identifies moments where visuals would improve reader retention.

🏷️

SEO Metadata

Every post is generated with complete meta titles, descriptions, canonical URLs, and structured data markup. Schema.org podcast episode data is included automatically so search engines understand your content's context.

🔌

WordPress Integration

Publish directly to your WordPress site with a single click. PodPress maps headings, images, categories, and tags to your WordPress setup, and supports custom post types and Advanced Custom Fields for complete flexibility.

📦

Bulk Export

Process your entire back catalog at once and export blog posts in Markdown, HTML, or WordPress XML format. Perfect for migrating content or building a searchable archive of every episode you have ever recorded.
