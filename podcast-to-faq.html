AI Question Detection

Our natural language processing engine listens to every episode and identifies questions posed by hosts, guests, and callers with remarkable precision. The model distinguishes between rhetorical questions, genuine inquiries, and audience-submitted prompts so only meaningful questions make it into your FAQ. Context windows ensure each detected question retains the surrounding discussion for accurate answer generation.

💡
Automated

Auto-Generated Answers

Once questions are identified, PodPress synthesizes clear, concise answers drawn directly from the episode transcript and any linked show notes. Each answer is attributed to the original speaker and timestamped so readers can jump straight to the source material. You can review, edit, and approve answers before they go live, giving you full editorial control over the final output.

PodPress uses a fine-tuned large language model trained on thousands of hours of podcast audio. It analyzes speech patterns, intonation cues from the transcript, and sentence structure to distinguish genuine questions from rhetorical ones, statements, and filler phrases. The system achieves over 94% precision on standard podcast formats.

You can upload MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, and OGG audio files directly, or provide an RSS feed URL for automatic ingestion. For transcripts, we accept SRT, VTT, TXT, and DOCX formats. If you do not have a transcript, PodPress will generate one using built-in speech-to-text before running the FAQ extraction pipeline.
