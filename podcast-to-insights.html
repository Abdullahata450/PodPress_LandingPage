Capabilities

Six ways to surface what matters most

📌

Key Takeaway Extraction

Automatically identify the most important points from each episode and present them as a prioritized list. The engine weighs speaker emphasis, topic recurrence, and audience engagement signals to rank takeaways by relevance.

💬

Quote Highlighting

Surface the most compelling quotes from hosts and guests, complete with timestamps and speaker attribution. Perfect for pulling social-ready soundbites or adding expert citations to blog posts and show notes.

🗂️

Topic Clustering

Group related discussion segments into coherent topic clusters so listeners can navigate directly to the subjects they care about. Each cluster includes a summary, time range, and list of contributing speakers.

📊

Sentiment Analysis

Understand the emotional arc of every conversation with segment-level sentiment scoring. Track how tone shifts across topics and identify moments of strong agreement, surprise, or debate between participants.

📝

Executive Summaries

Generate concise, paragraph-length summaries tailored for busy professionals who want the gist without listening to the full episode. Summaries include key decisions, action items, and notable data points mentioned in the discussion.

🃏

Shareable Cards

Transform insights into beautifully designed visual cards optimized for social media, email, and Slack. Each card includes the quote or takeaway, your podcast branding, and a direct link back to the episode.
