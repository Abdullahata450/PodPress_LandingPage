What you can do

Step 01

Upload or connect podcast episodes

Drop an audio file or paste your RSS feed URL. Podpress syncs episode data, titles, and audio automatically — no manual setup needed.

Step 02

Generate transcripts automatically

Step 03

Create newsletters from episode content

Structured, publish-ready email drafts generated directly from your transcript.

Edit tone, trim length, and apply your brand voice — AI suggestions appear inline so you can polish in seconds, not hours.
