How It Works

Transform one episode into a content empire

🔄

One-to-Many Engine

Feed in a single podcast episode and our AI instantly generates multiple content assets tailored for different platforms. The engine analyzes your audio for key moments, quotable insights, and narrative arcs to produce blog posts, social clips, newsletters, and more. Every output preserves your original voice and messaging while adapting tone and length for each channel.

AI-Powered
🎯

Format Intelligence

Not all platforms are equal, and neither should your content be. Format Intelligence understands the unique constraints and best practices of each distribution channel automatically. It adjusts character counts for Twitter, aspect ratios for Instagram, and reading levels for blog content so every piece feels native to its destination.

Smart Formatting
🌐

Cross-Platform Publishing

Once your content is generated, publish it everywhere from a single dashboard. Connect your WordPress, Medium, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and email marketing accounts for one-click distribution. Schedule posts across time zones and track engagement metrics from every platform in one unified analytics view.

Multi-Channel
Output Formats

Every format your audience consumes

📝

Blog Posts

Transform your episode into a fully structured blog article complete with headers, key takeaways, and SEO metadata. Each post is optimized for readability with natural paragraph breaks and internal linking suggestions. Publish directly to WordPress, Ghost, or export as Markdown.

🎬

Social Clips

Automatically identify the most engaging 30- to 90-second segments from your episode and export them as video clips. Each clip is captioned, cropped for vertical or square formats, and ready to upload to TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts.

✉️

Email Newsletters

Generate a polished newsletter recap of your episode with highlighted quotes, key discussion points, and embedded audio snippets. Templates are mobile-responsive and compatible with Mailchimp, ConvertKit, and Substack out of the box.

💬

Quote Cards

Extract the most shareable quotes from your transcript and render them as beautifully designed image cards. Choose from dozens of templates that match your brand colors and typography. Download as PNG or share directly to social media platforms.

🧵

Thread Generator

Convert long-form discussions into engaging Twitter/X threads that break down complex topics into bite-sized posts. The generator adds hooks, numbering, and call-to-actions automatically. Preview and edit each tweet before scheduling or publishing the full thread.

📋

Show Notes

Produce comprehensive show notes with timestamped topic summaries, guest bios, resource links, and key takeaways. Show notes are formatted for your podcast hosting platform and optimized with relevant keywords to improve discoverability in search engines.
