How It Works

From video to inbox in minutes

🎬
Extraction

Auto-Extract Key Points

Paste any YouTube URL and our AI watches the entire video, identifying the most important moments, arguments, and insights. It pulls out timestamped key points and organizes them into a logical narrative structure that reads naturally as a newsletter. No manual note-taking or scrubbing through footage required.

✨
Formatting

Smart Visual Formatting

Your newsletter is automatically enriched with thumbnail screenshots, chapter markers, and embedded video links at the right moments. The layout adapts to your brand template, inserting pull quotes from the most compelling moments and creating scannable sections that mirror the video's structure. Readers get the full value without watching a single frame.

Capabilities

Powerful tools for YouTube creators

📥

Video Import

Import videos directly by URL, channel feed, or playlist. PodPress connects to your YouTube account and can automatically process new uploads as they go live, keeping your newsletter pipeline always full.

💬

Caption Extraction

Pulls captions from YouTube's auto-generated or manually uploaded subtitle tracks with high accuracy. Supports over 40 languages and handles multi-speaker identification for interview-style content.

🧠

Smart Summarization

Our AI condenses hour-long videos into concise, newsletter-length summaries without losing critical context. It preserves the speaker's voice and tone while trimming tangents and repetition for a tighter read.

🖼️

Visual Thumbnails

Automatically captures the best frames from your video to use as newsletter imagery. Select from AI-suggested thumbnails or upload your own to maintain visual consistency across your brand.

⚡

Bulk Processing

Queue up an entire playlist or backlog of videos and process them all at once. PodPress handles batch conversion with parallel processing, so even large libraries are ready within minutes.

📡

Multi-Channel

Manage multiple YouTube channels from a single dashboard. Each channel can have its own newsletter template, subscriber list, and delivery schedule for complete creative separation.
