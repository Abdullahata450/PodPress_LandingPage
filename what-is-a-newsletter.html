What Is a Podcast Newsletter?

A podcast newsletter is an email sent to subscribers that summarizes, highlights, or expands on a podcast episode. It transforms spoken content into structured written communication.

Instead of show notes, a podcast newsletter delivers curated episode insights directly to your audience's inbox.

How is a podcast newsletter different from show notes?

Show Notes

  • Posted on episode pages
  • Often brief
  • Primarily SEO-focused

Podcast Newsletter

  • Sent via email
  • Structured and narrative
  • Designed for subscriber engagement
  • Highlights insights, key themes, and context

Why podcasters use newsletters

  • ✓Build direct audience relationships
  • ✓Increase engagement beyond listening
  • ✓Reach subscribers who don't listen weekly
  • ✓Reinforce episode themes
  • ✓Create a repeatable content channel

What does a podcast newsletter include?

A typical podcast newsletter may include:

Episode introduction Key insights Important quotes Segment breakdown Call-to-action
