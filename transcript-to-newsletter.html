How it works

Feed your transcript into PodPress and instantly turn spoken content into an email-ready newsletter draft with structure, clarity, and flow.

Step 01

Upload your episode or content

Bring in a transcript, audio file, or episode source and PodPress prepares the text for a newsletter workflow without extra formatting work.

Step 02

AI transcript generated

Need a transcript first? PodPress can transcribe your episode automatically, organize the copy, and clean up the raw conversation for publishing.

Step 03

Transcript becomes newsletter

The transcript is transformed into a polished draft with subject-ready sections, highlights, summaries, and a structure built for reader engagement.

Step 04

Segment, refine, or customize

Adjust the tone, trim sections, add links and CTAs, or shape the final newsletter to match your brand before publishing anywhere you send email.

Why podcasters use transcript-based newsletters

Turn every transcript into one more audience touchpoint. PodPress helps you create cleaner recaps, faster publishing workflows, and more value from each episode you already record.

What you can generate

Choose the format that fits your audience. From concise summaries to richer editorial recaps, your transcript can become multiple newsletter styles in a few clicks.

A

Short email summaries and highlights

Create concise newsletter intros that explain what happened in the episode and give readers the fastest path to the core takeaways.

Q

Key insights and talking points

Pull out the strongest ideas, quotable moments, and audience-ready takeaways so each newsletter feels thoughtful and easy to skim.

W

Daily, weekly, or episode recaps

Turn one transcript or multiple episodes into regular roundup newsletters that keep subscribers engaged between releases.

S

SEO-friendly newsletter drafts

Generate structured copy with searchable phrasing, helpful headings, and reusable content blocks you can also adapt for archives and web publishing.
