How to Turn a Podcast Into a Newsletter

Turning a podcast into a newsletter helps you reach more of your audience by using your podcast transcripts as the base for structured email content.

Podpress helps you convert podcast episodes into newsletters automatically using AI.

Why turn a podcast into a newsletter?

  • ✓Reach audiences who don’t listen to full episodes
  • ✓Build an owned email list
  • ✓Extend the lifespan of each episode
  • ✓Reinforce key insights in written format
  • ✓Create consistent weekly publishing

Step-by-step: turning a podcast into a newsletter

Step 01

Generate a transcript

The transcript becomes the source of truth for insights, quotes, and ideas from the episode.

  • YouTube podcast links
  • Podcast RSS feeds
  • Uploaded MP3 audio files
Step 02

Structure the content

Instead of dumping raw text into an email, the content should be logically structured for reading.

  • Clear introduction
  • Key themes or segments
  • Highlights or insights
  • Closing summary
Podpress automatically transforms transcripts into structured newsletters based on the content flow.
Step 03

Choose a newsletter format

Multiple newsletter styles are available to match your audience's preferences.

Short Summary Detailed Long-form
Regeneration is possible within plan limits if you want to try a different format.
Step 04

Refine or repurpose

After generation, you have several options to perfect the content:

  • Use AI refinement tools
  • Regenerate in a different format
  • Generate additional template outputs
  • Download in TXT or SRT format
