Transcript Generator

Convert audio and video into accurate, speaker-labeled transcripts in minutes

Core Features

Everything you need for flawless transcripts

🎙️

Speech-to-Text Engine

Our proprietary AI engine converts spoken audio into written text with remarkable precision. Trained on millions of hours of podcast audio, it handles accents, jargon, and fast-paced dialogue with ease. Simply upload your file and let the engine do the rest.

👥

Speaker Identification

Automatically detect and label different speakers throughout your recording. The system learns voice patterns to distinguish between hosts, guests, and callers without manual tagging. Each speaker gets a consistent label across the entire transcript.

⏱️

Timestamp Sync

Every sentence is precisely synced with its corresponding audio timestamp. Click any line in the transcript to jump directly to that moment in the recording. Perfect for creating show notes, pull quotes, and audiograms with exact timing.

🌍

Multi-Language Support

Transcribe content in over 40 languages with native-level accuracy. Our models are fine-tuned for each language's unique phonetic patterns and colloquialisms. Seamlessly handle multilingual episodes where speakers switch between languages mid-conversation.

📄

Export Formats

Download your finished transcripts in the format that fits your workflow best. Choose from plain text, SRT, VTT, Word documents, or structured JSON for developer integrations. Every export preserves speaker labels, timestamps, and paragraph formatting.

⚡

Batch Processing

Upload an entire season of episodes and transcribe them all at once. Our queue system processes files in parallel so you get results faster than sequential uploads. Monitor progress in real time and receive notifications when each transcript is ready.
