Podcast Transcript Generator

Generate accurate podcast transcripts from your episodes using AI. Podpress converts YouTube podcasts, RSS feeds, or uploaded audio files into full transcripts that power structured newsletter creation.

Supported inputs

Podpress supports:

  • 1 YouTube podcast links
  • 2 Podcast RSS feed URLs
  • 3 Uploaded MP3 audio files

Once processed, your transcript is always accessible in the Transcript tab.

Why transcripts matter for podcasters

A transcript allows you to:

  • Search episode content
  • Extract quotes and insights
  • Repurpose content into newsletters
  • Maintain accuracy when writing

Podpress uses the transcript as the source of truth for all generated outputs.

What happens after transcription?

Your transcript can be transformed into:

  • AI-generated podcast newsletters
  • Key insights
  • Blog-style summaries
  • Discussion questions
  • SEO-friendly content snippets

All outputs are derived from the same transcript and episode context.

Built for podcasters

Podpress is not a general AI transcription tool. It is built exclusively for podcasters who want to turn episodes into structured written content.
